About This Counseling Session

Common PGDCA Student Concerns

We address the most frequent issues faced by IGNOU PGDCA students

Are you confused about the re-evaluation process?

Many students receive lower marks than expected or even fail certain papers, leaving them unsure about how to proceed with re-evaluation.

The re-evaluation process at IGNOU can be confusing - knowing when to apply, how to fill out the form correctly, what fees to pay, and understanding the timeline for results.

Our session will provide step-by-step guidance on the entire re-evaluation process

Submitted assignments months ago but marks not updated?

It's a common frustration among IGNOU students - you submitted your assignments on time, but months later, the portal still shows "not submitted" or "not updated."

Without updated assignment marks, you can't be sure of your standing, and it creates anxiety about whether your submission was received and evaluated properly.

We'll explain the assignment evaluation system and what steps to take when marks aren't updated

How are marks calculated with lab and practicals?

Understanding how IGNOU calculates your final marks - combining theory exam scores, assignment marks, and practical/lab scores - is confusing for many PGDCA students.

Without clarity on this calculation, it's difficult to know where you stand academically and what to focus on for improvement.

We'll break down the mark calculation formula and help you understand your scorecard

Uncertain about re-exam processes and submission deadlines?

Many students miss critical deadlines for exam forms, re-registration, and re-evaluation applications, delaying their progress by entire semesters.

Keeping track of multiple deadlines while managing studies is challenging, and confusing re-exam with re-evaluation processes leads to missed opportunities.

We'll clarify all processes and provide a timeline with reminders so you never miss a deadline.

Are you also interested to know about STET exam and its syllabus?

Many PGDCA students aspire to become computer teachers in Bihar government schools but are confused about the STET examination process.

Students often struggle with understanding eligibility criteria, exam pattern, syllabus coverage, and preparation strategy for the Computer Science paper of Bihar STET.

We'll cover STET eligibility requirements, detailed syllabus, preparation strategy, and previous year question patterns to help you qualify.
